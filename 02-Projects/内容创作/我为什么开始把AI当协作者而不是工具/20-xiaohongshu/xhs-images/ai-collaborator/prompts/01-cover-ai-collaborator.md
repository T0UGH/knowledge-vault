Create a Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book) style infographic following these guidelines:

## Image Specifications
- Type: Infographic cover
- Orientation: Portrait (vertical)
- Aspect Ratio: 3:4
- Style: screen-print editorial illustration

## Core Principles
- Screen print / silkscreen poster art with light poster feeling
- 2-4 flat colors maximum, no realistic rendering
- Strong visual impact but highly readable Chinese text
- Use negative space actively, keep composition simple and bold
- All text should feel hand-drawn poster lettering, not digital UI text

## Color Rules
- Prefer light poster palette: warm cream background, deep teal + burnt orange as main contrast, small crimson accents
- Use halftone dot texture and subtle paper grain
- Avoid cute or overly playful mood

## Layout
- Sparse cover composition
- One large statement, one smaller subtitle
- Big title dominates upper-middle area
- Main symbolic illustration in lower half

## Content
- Main title: 把 AI 当工具，已经不够了
- Subtitle: 我为什么开始把 AI 当协作者
- Visual concept: a person on the left faces a connected network of collaboration nodes on the right; the old single tool icon fades away, replaced by an active collaborative system
- Mood: personal insight, clear viewpoint, modern editorial poster

## Text Language
- Use Chinese only
- Ensure title is large and instantly readable on mobile
